About Me

I am a senior undergraduate in Computer Science at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, with a Spring 2025 visiting experience at UC Berkeley. My recent work focuses on machine learning for electronic design automation, especially surrogate modeling, uncertainty-aware Bayesian optimization, and data-driven analog circuit sizing.

Alongside research, I enjoy building systems close to the hardware stack, including operating systems, processor architecture, compiler toolchains, and FPGA-based implementation. I am interested in using data-efficient machine learning methods to improve the speed and reliability of scientific and engineering workflows.
